Why Encrypted Voices?

Digital privacy isn’t optional — it’s essential. Every click, message, and download can leave a trace.
Without strong global protections, personal data is vulnerable to theft, surveillance, and manipulation.
Encrypted Voices was created to educate, empower, and push for stronger international laws.

What We Stand For

● Advocating global digital privacy rights

● Exposing real-life stories from students and youth

● Teaching people how to protect themselves online

● Uniting voices to demand policy change

Naive. Desperate. Targeted.

I was just trying to find work. An email came through our school account offering a student assistant job. It looked legit—same school logo, same tone. I clicked. I filled out the form. Then the messages started coming in.

Later, we all got an announcement: our school's system had been breached. It wasn’t just me. So many of us had been tricked. That moment changed how I saw the internet—it's not just websites and fun, it's risk and responsibility.

I created Encrypted Voices so others won’t fall like we did.
